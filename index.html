Skills & Methods

研究技能与方法

围绕资源环境经济、能源转型与流域协同治理，形成“理论建模 + 计量识别 + 可解释机器学习 + 空间数据处理”的复合方法能力。

My methodological toolkit combines theoretical modeling, causal inference, interpretable machine learning, and spatial-environmental data analysis for resource and environmental economics.

StataPythonMATLABArcGISLaTeX DID / DDDRDIV / 2SLSPanel Data River Network ModelingSocial Planner ModelEcological Compensation Coupling Coordination DegreeEntropy Weight Method CNN-BiLSTM-AttentionSHAPInterpretable Machine Learning

资源环境理论分析

能够围绕资源约束、生态外部性与区域协同治理构建分析框架，刻画要素配置、福利变化与不同主体之间的利益分配关系。

I develop analytical frameworks for resource constraints, ecological externalities, and regional coordination, with attention to factor allocation, welfare changes, and distributional effects across stakeholders.

生态治理与机制设计

关注生态保护成本、受益分配、补偿机制与激励相容问题，能够从外部性内部化和多主体协调角度分析环境治理政策。

I analyze ecological protection costs, benefit sharing, compensation mechanisms, and incentive compatibility from the perspectives of externality internalization and multi-stakeholder coordination.

计量识别与政策评估

熟悉城市、县域和企业层面面板数据处理，能够运用准自然实验方法评估政策效应，并开展稳健性检验、异质性分析和机制检验。

I work with city-, county-, and firm-level panel data, applying quasi-experimental methods for policy evaluation, robustness checks, heterogeneity analysis, and mechanism tests.

可解释机器学习

能够将机器学习模型与可解释方法结合，用于识别关键驱动因素、非线性关系、阈值特征和变量交互，并与传统计量分析形成互证。

I combine machine learning models with explainable methods to identify key drivers, nonlinear relationships, threshold patterns, and interaction effects, and to cross-validate them with econometric evidence.

综合评价与空间数据

能够开展多指标综合评价、协调关系测度和空间环境数据处理，综合使用统计年鉴、遥感影像、环境监测和地理空间数据支撑城市与流域尺度研究。

I conduct multi-indicator evaluation, coordination measurement, and spatial-environmental data processing, integrating statistical yearbooks, remote sensing products, environmental monitoring data, and geospatial datasets.

工具与学术服务

常用 Stata、Python、MATLAB、ArcGIS 和 LaTeX 进行数据清洗、计量分析、模型仿真、空间可视化与论文写作。担任 Energy Efficiency（SCI Q2）、Global Development Dialogues 等期刊审稿人。

I use Stata, Python, MATLAB, ArcGIS, and LaTeX for data cleaning, econometric analysis, model simulation, spatial visualization, and academic writing. I also serve as a reviewer for Energy Efficiency and Global Development Dialogues.
